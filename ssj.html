Goku & Vegeta: Saiyan Origins & Transformations

Goku's Origin

Son Goku, originally named Kakarot, is a Saiyan sent to Earth as a baby. Raised by Grandpa Gohan, Goku grew up unaware of his alien heritage. He is known for his cheerful personality, insatiable appetite, and incredible fighting spirit. Goku's Saiyan bloodline grants him immense strength and the ability to grow stronger after every battle.

Vegeta's Origin

Vegeta is the prince of the Saiyan race and heir to the Saiyan throne. Raised on Planet Vegeta, he was trained as a warrior from birth and initially a ruthless and proud fighter. Over time, Vegeta's rivalry with Goku and his own personal growth led him to become a hero and protector of Earth.

Goku's Transformations

Super Saiyan 3

Red hair and aura, divine ki and godly power.

Super Saiyan Blue

super saiyan blue kaioken

Silver hair and eyes, ultimate form of instinct and power, on par with some gods of destructions.
